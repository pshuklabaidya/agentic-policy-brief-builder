## Reform Options

### Option A: Missing-Middle Housing Overlay

Allow duplexes, triplexes, fourplexes, courtyard apartments, and townhomes on residential lots within one-half mile of frequent transit stops. Projects that meet published form standards would receive ministerial approval.

### Option B: Accessory Dwelling Unit Streamlining

Permit one internal or detached accessory dwelling unit on every residential lot. Cap city review at 45 days for complete applications and publish pre-approved design plans for homeowners.

### Option C: Parking Flexibility

Remove minimum off-street parking requirements within transit-rich areas and let project sponsors decide how much parking to provide. Maintain accessible parking requirements and curb-management tools for loading, deliveries, and disability access.

### Option D: Affordability Incentive

Offer a modest height or floor-area bonus for projects that reserve at least 15% of homes for households earning below 80% of area median income.
