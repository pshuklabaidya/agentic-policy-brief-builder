# Screenshot Guide

Do not commit screenshots or generated assets unless they are intentionally small, useful, and approved. For most portfolio updates, capture screenshots externally and place them in the profile README, project page, or portfolio site instead of this repository.

## Recommended screenshots

1. **App landing state**
   - Caption: “Agentic Policy Brief Builder Streamlit interface with synthetic packet and upload options.”
   - Include the document-source selector and policy-question input.

2. **Synthetic packet selection**
   - Caption: “Safe public demo path using a fictional synthetic Riverton policy packet.”
   - Make sure the caption discloses synthetic data.

3. **Generation status panel**
   - Caption: “Workflow status showing document loading, chunking, indexing, retrieval, drafting, and citation audit.”

4. **Retrieved evidence display**
   - Caption: “Ranked evidence chunks with stable evidence IDs for reviewer inspection.”
   - Avoid exposing private uploaded files or confidential text.

5. **Generated policy brief**
   - Caption: “Structured brief draft with findings, options, risks/tradeoffs, recommendation, and evidence IDs.”

6. **Citation audit display**
   - Caption: “Citation audit checks whether cited evidence IDs map back to retrieved evidence.”

7. **Markdown download button**
   - Caption: “Markdown export packages the draft, retrieved evidence, and audit context for review.”

8. **CI or local validation output**
   - Caption: “Quality gates run linting, tests, deterministic evals, repository-health checks, and release-readiness checks.”

## Synthetic-data disclosure guidance

Use captions such as:

- “Demo shown with fictional synthetic Riverton policy data.”
- “Synthetic data is used for safe public demonstration and does not prove real-world policy accuracy.”
- “No private customer, employer, confidential, or production policy records are shown.”

Avoid captions that imply production deployment, legal advice, policy correctness, or operational readiness.
